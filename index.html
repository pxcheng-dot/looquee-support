Common Questions

Why are recommendations personalized?

Looquee uses details such as interests, academic records, goals, school preferences, and saved colleges to provide more relevant suggestions.

Can Looquee guarantee admission?

No. Looquee provides guidance and organization tools, but admission decisions are made by colleges and universities.

What should I do if something looks wrong?

Email us with a short description, your device model, iOS version, and screenshots if possible.
